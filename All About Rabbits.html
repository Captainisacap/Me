Basic Information

Rabbits are small Mammals in the family of Leporidie. The same family of Pika and Hare.

Their Kingdom is Animalia. Male rabbits are called bucks; females are called does. An older term for an adult rabbit is coney, while rabbit once referred only to the young animals. Mostly Rabbits are called Bunnies and are kept as pets.
